My Experience in Ushimado

Visiting Ushimado was more than just a trip for me — it was a chance to connect with nature, people, and culture in a very warm and peaceful way.

From the calm sea of the Seto Inland Sea to the kindness of the local community, every moment felt special and meaningful.

Through activities, cultural exchange, and daily life experiences, Ushimado became a place I will always remember.
